Lead Management

Multi-Branch Loan Processing System

A multi-location mortgage brokerage was receiving leads across 4 different office locations.

n8nActiveCampaignAI Value NodeCalendlyGoogle DocsSMS
The Problem

What needed to be solved

A multi-location mortgage brokerage was receiving leads across 4 different office locations. Each lead required manual sorting by location, CRM entry, document creation, calendar booking, and SMS confirmation — a process taking 15–20 minutes per lead and prone to routing errors.

What I Built

The automation system

I built a comprehensive end-to-end lead processing system. Leads from multiple sources are merged, phone formats standardised, contacts and deals created in the CRM, an AI node scores each lead's value, routes by loan type, sends SMS confirmation, checks Calendly, creates location-specific Google Docs, and logs everything back to the CRM.

Results

What this delivered

↑

Lead processing time reduced from 15–20 mins to under 30 seconds

✓

Zero routing errors across 4 branch locations

⚡

Every lead gets an instant SMS and booking link

◆

AI scoring helps brokers prioritise high-value leads first

Bottom Line

The brokerage went from manually handling every lead to a fully automated pipeline that processes and routes leads across 4 locations without any staff involvement.
